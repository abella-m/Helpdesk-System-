User Management Form

* Click User management and you will see two options, the add users and view list.

* To Add users you will click the add users button and it will redirect you to a form that you need to fiil up with the following information: ID Number, Lastname, Firstname, Middlename, Email, Category, Password and Confirm password.. After finishing you will click the add button at the right side bottom of the form to successfully add the user. There is also a clear button to delete when you type a wrong information. To add multiple users, click Add multiple button to save a multiple of users, upon clicking it you will need to import data of a certain excel file. Then click the upload button to insert your excel file.




* To View list, click the view list button and you can see all the list of users that has been added. You can update the information and you can also delete a certain user.
