Manage Queries Form

* Click the Queries button to manage queries, where in you can view and answer the queries of the users. Click the view button to see and answer the query. And click the remark button to see the remarks of user.

* Upon clicking the eye icon button you will see the information of the student and their queries then you will answer it there already and click the submit button at the right side bottom of the form. A message will pop-up and will tell you that the query has been answered successfully.

*Upon clicking the second icon button you will see the remarks of the user if they have satisfied by the answer in their query.
